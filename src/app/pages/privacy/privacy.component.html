D. COOKIES AND OTHER TECHNOLOGIES

Stage uses technologies called cookies, to collect information about the pages you view, the links you click and other actions you take on Stage’s website and services.Cookies are small text files that are stored on your hard drive through your web browser.

Stage. may use cookies in order to store your preferences and other information on your computer in order to save time by eliminating the need to repeatedly enter the same information, for example by remembering log in information when you use Stage’s Apps. However, the cookies used on Stage’s websites do not contain information that identifies you personally.

1. COOKIES IN APPS

Stage uses session cookies as well as persistent cookies on its websites. A session cookie is a temporary cookie which is erased when a session is ended, i.e. usually when you close your browser, while a persistent cookie is stored in one of your browsers subfolders until you delete it manually using your browser “delete cookies” setting or when the persistent cookie is set to be deleted automatically.

Examples of persistent cookies Stage uses are cookies to determine whether to show “sign up” or “log in” when prompting a user to log in and/or in order to remember information about a logged in user.

1. THIRD PARTY COOKIES IN APPS

Stage also uses third party cookies. We use third party cookies to understand how our Apps are being used, whether our Apps are working as they are intended, and understanding user’s interaction with the IP. Currently, our third-party service provider is Google Analytics. These cookies just provide us information on what pages you are looking at, what items on the pages you are clicking on, how much time you are spending on a page and other types of information that help us to understand how you are using our website and the pages and content in the website. The information collected through these third-party services may also be used by the third-party service. Please see the service privacy policy for more information.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your settings to decline cookies as well as delete existing cookies from your device if you prefer. If you choose to decline and/or delete cookies, you may not be able to use all features of Stage’s websites orservices that depend on cookies.
