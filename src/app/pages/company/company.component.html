Hello. We're Stage, Inc.

Our mission is to bring people together for shared experiences.

We are Stage Inc., an entertainment technology company and creator of Stageverse, a new virtual world for creative pioneers to make magic without limits, hosting unforgettable immersive shared experiences that bring people together through any mobile device - coming soon to desktop and virtual reality.

As the lines become blurred between our online and offline worlds, Stageverse is closing the gap between physical and virtual spaces and building a playground for creators and their communities to create the next generation of experiences.

We're a completely distributed passionate team of creative technologists, engineers, architects, musicians, SFX artists, character designers, film-makers, game designers and marketers. Currently based out of Los Angeles and Sydney.
